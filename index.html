who is scusal?

We are an organization for students who study, make, and appreciate visual art. With the goal of reinvigorating a culture of the arts on campus and beyond, our organization connects students with resources, events, and opportunities. We hope to give students the agency to interact with art, whether that be through direct involvement in the art-making process or by critically engaging with artistic discourse.

We host 2-3 events a quarter from group shows to maker nights to field trips !
